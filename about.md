This blog covers solution architecture and enterprise architecture in practice: design principles, methodologies (TOGAF, ADM, C4, arc42, and others), decision frameworks, trade-off analysis, and lessons pulled from real systems rather than slideware.

The goal is to write the kind of post a working architect would actually bookmark — concrete, opinionated where it counts, and honest about trade-offs.
