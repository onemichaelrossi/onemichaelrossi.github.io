This blog exists to think through solution architecture and enterprise architecture out loud: the principles that hold up across projects, the methodologies and frameworks worth knowing (and their limits), and the trade-offs that don't make it into the reference diagrams.

Posts here will lean toward concrete decisions over abstract theory — the kind of thing you'd want on hand the next time you're arguing about a target-state diagram or picking between two integration patterns.
